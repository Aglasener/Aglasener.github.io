The Aphid

The aphid is an astonishing creature. It is both a focus of fascination for entmologists and a bane to gardners and farmers. Their adaptability and symbiotic relationships, have allowed them to flourish across the world.

Two examples of their capabilities are: their ability to asexually produce, creating clones of themselves when no mates are available, and to produce offspring with wings when a trigger is tripped by environmental factors
